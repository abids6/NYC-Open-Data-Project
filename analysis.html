Bus Delay Analysis

This chart shows the number of school bus delay records by borough based on the cards used in this project.

What The Chart Means

This chart shows that Brooklyn and Queens have the most bus delay records in my sample, with 2 records each. Bronx and Staten Island each have 1 record. This does not mean Brooklyn and Queens always have the most school bus delays. It only shows what appears in the small group of bus delay cards used for this project.

Delay Status Chart

What The Second Chart Means

The second chart shows that most of the records are marked as Running Late. There are also some records marked as Breakdown. This means the bus problems were not all the same. Some buses were late, while other buses had bigger problems like mechanical issues or not starting.

Conclusion

From these records, school bus delays can happen for different reasons and in different boroughs. The cards show the details, the map shows the locations, and the charts make the records easier to compare.
