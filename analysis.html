What The Chart Means

The chart shows that Brooklyn and Queens have the most bus delay records in my sample. They each have two records. Bronx and Staten Island each have one record. This does not mean Brooklyn and Queens always have the most bus delays. It only shows what appears in the small group of cards I used for this project.

What The Second Chart Means

The second chart shows that most of the records are marked as Running Late. There are also some records marked as Breakdown. This means the bus problems were not all the same. Some buses were late, while other buses had bigger problems like mechanical issues or not starting.

Conclusion

From these records, school bus delays can happen for different reasons and in different boroughs. The cards help show the details of each record, and the charts make it easier to compare the records.
