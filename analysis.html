What The Chart Shows

Brooklyn and Queens each have two delay records in this sample. Bronx and Staten Island each have one delay record.

This does not mean Brooklyn and Queens always have the most school bus delays. It only shows what appears in the small sample used for this project.

Conclusion

The records show that school bus problems can include both running late and actual bus breakdowns. Some reasons include traffic, mechanical problems, other issues, and buses that would not start.
