What I Looked At

I looked at a small sample of school bus delay records. I focused on the borough, reason for delay, and status of each record.

What I Noticed

Brooklyn and Queens showed up more than once in the sample. Bronx and Staten Island also showed up, but only one time each.

Some delays were marked as Running Late, and some were marked as Breakdown. This means not every record is the same type of problem.

Reasons For Delays

Some reasons in the records were Heavy Traffic, Mechanical Problem, Other, and Won't Start. Heavy traffic makes sense because school buses can get stuck on busy roads.

Conclusion

From this sample, school bus delays can happen in different boroughs and for different reasons. The map on the View / Filter page helps show where the delay records are located.
