What The Chart Shows

The chart shows that Brooklyn and Queens have the most records in this small sample. Bronx and Staten Island each appear once. This does not mean those boroughs always have the most delays, but it shows what appears in the data used for this project.

Delay Reasons

Some reasons in the data include Heavy Traffic, Mechanical Problem, Other, and Won't Start. This shows that school bus delays can happen for different reasons.
