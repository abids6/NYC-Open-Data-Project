Background

This website uses the School Bus Breakdown and Delays dataset from NYC Open Data. The dataset shows school bus delays and breakdowns in New York City.

The data is provided by the Department of Education because it is about school buses, students, routes, bus companies, and delay reports.

About The Dataset

The dataset includes information such as the borough, route number, bus number, run type, delay reason, delay time, students on the bus, status, and bus company.

Why This Data Matters

This data matters because school bus delays can affect students, families, schools, and bus companies. A delay can make students late and can also help show common problems with school transportation.

What This Website Provides

The View / Filter page shows the school bus delay records as cards. Users can filter the cards by borough or by status. The Analysis page shows a simple chart and explains what the sample data shows.
