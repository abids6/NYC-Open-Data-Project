Background

This website uses the School Bus Breakdown and Delays dataset from NYC Open Data. The dataset is connected to school bus delay reports in New York City.

The agency connected to this dataset is the Department of Education. This makes sense because the data is about school buses, students, routes, bus companies, and delay reports.

About The Dataset

The dataset includes information such as the borough, bus route, bus number, reason for the delay, how long the bus was delayed, students on the bus, and the bus company.

Why This Data Matters

School bus delays matter because students, families, schools, and bus companies can all be affected when a bus is late or breaks down. Looking at this data can help people understand where delays happen and what causes them.

What This Website Provides

The View / Filter page shows the bus delay data in card form. Users can filter the cards by borough or by delay status. The Analysis page shows a simple chart and explains what the chart shows.
