Background

This website uses the School Bus Breakdown and Delays dataset from NYC Open Data. The data shows school bus delays and breakdowns in New York City.

The data is connected to the Department of Education because it is about school buses, students, routes, delay reports, and bus companies.

About The Data

The records include the borough, route number, bus number, run type, reason for the delay, how long the bus was delayed, number of students on the bus, status, and bus company.

Bus Delay Pictures

Why I Picked This Data

I picked this dataset because school bus delays can affect students, parents, schools, and bus companies. It is useful to see where delays happen and what some of the reasons are.

Website Pages

The View / Filter page shows bus delay cards and a map with markers. The Analysis page has a chart made from the cards and explains what the chart means.
